The Golden Valley Cardinal Regime has one of the top band programs in the California region. Our marching band has participated in the Pasadena Rose Parade, Disneyland parade, and does many more performances from the colorguard, percussion, and jazz ensembles throughout the year. Every year we host the Central California Band Review, a competition for both nearby and far away high school and middle school bands.
