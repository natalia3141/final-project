The Golden Valley Cardinal Regime has had a tradition of excellence in the music arts for decades. Every year we host the Central California Band Review, a competition for both nearby and far away high school and middle school bands.

Along with our marching band, we have 3 jazz ensembles, percussion group, colorguard group, and 2 concert bands. Most students in the program are a part of two or more of these groups. They spend time every day during school and outside of school hours to practice and improve.
